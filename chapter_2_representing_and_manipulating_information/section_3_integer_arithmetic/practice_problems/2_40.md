# Practice 2.40

For each of the following values of K, find ways to express `x * K` using only the specified number of operations, where we consider both additions and subtractions to have comparable cost. You may need to use some tricks beyond the simple form A and B rules we have considered so far.

| Row     | K           | Shifts      | Add/Subs | Expression
| ------- | ----------- | ----------- | -------- | ---------------
| [A.](#a)| 7           | 1           | 1        |  ___       
| [B.](#b)| 30          | 4           | 3        |  ___     
| [C.](#c)| 28          | 2           | 1        |  ___      
| [D.](#d)| 55          | 2           | 2        |  ___      

---

## A.
```
K      = 7
Shifts = 1
Ops    = 1
---
How can we express K as powers of 2?
K = 2^2 + 2^1 + 2^0 = 4 + 2 + 1 = 7 (Addition Only)
K = 2^3 - 2^0 = 8 - 1 = 7 (Subtraction Only)

Looks like the subtration case gets us close to the required amount of shifts and operations, so the expression could look like (1 Shift, 1 Op):
x*7 = (x<<3) - (x<<0)
    = (x<<3) - x      
```

## B.
```
K      = 30
Shifts = 4
Ops    = 3
---
How can we express K as powers of 2?
K = 2^4 + 2^3 + 2^2 + 2^1 = 16 + 8 + 4 + 2 = 30 (Addition Only)
K = 2^5 - 2^1 = 32 - 2 = 30 (Subtraction Only)

Looks like addition case gets us to the required amount of shifts and operations (4 Shifts, 3 Ops):
x*30 = (x<<4) + (x<<3) + (x<<2) + (x<<1)    
```

## C.
```
K      = 28
Shifts = 2
Ops    = 1
---
How can we express K as powers of 2?
K = 2^4 + 2^3 + 2^2 = 16 + 8 + 4 = 28 (Addition Only)
K = 2^5 - 2^2 = 32 - 4 = 28 (Subtraction Only)

Looks like the subtraction case gets us to the required amount of shifts and operations (2 Shifts, 1 Op):

```

## D.
```
K      = 55
Shifts = 2
Ops    = 2
---
How can we express K as powers of 2?
K = 2^5 + 2^4 + 2^2 + 2^1 + 2^0 = 32 + 16 + 4 + 2 + 1 = 55 (Addition Only)
K = 2^6 - 2^3 - 2^0 = 64 - 8 - 1 = 55 (Subtraction Only)

Looks like the subtraction case gets us to the required amount of shifts and operations (2 Shifts, 2 Ops):
x*55 = (x<<6) - (x<<3) - x
```
